BASED PAPER MOST COOKING IN PAPER LIL B GOES SO HARD IN THIS ONE is a paper I wrote explaining the importance of the popular but controversial rapper Lil B through the lens of postmodernism.

Lil B᾿s work is extremely contentious because he doesn᾿t follow fundamental norms present in rap music. I argue that he is a deconstructionist, deliberately challenging Hip-Hop as a form, and thereby making an extremely important contribution to the genre.
