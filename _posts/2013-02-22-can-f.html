can-f is a music video I made for Charlie Abbott (AKA RYV) in February, 2013 to coincide with the release of the Bedroom Classics Vol. 1 compilation on Belgian Man Records.

I created the video using CoGe and Unity3d, using audio-synced timeline information piped in over OSC.
